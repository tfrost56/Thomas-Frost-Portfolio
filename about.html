About Me

I'm a self taught web developer based in Oxfordshire, UK. Since December 2019 I have taken it upon myself to learn web development alongside my full time job as a Travel Consulatant. The ultimate goal is to move away from travel and move in to the web industry.

So far my journey has been fixated on Front-end technologies, having recently finished an an advanced CSS and Sass course I am now planning to focus more on Javascript and the React Framework. Beyond that I intend to venture into the backend and do a deep dive into C# and .NET with the intention to keep expanding my knowledge. Over the past 6 months it has become clear that I have a real passion for Web Development and plan to keep improving my code and add more technologies over time. Having had 5 years as a travel Consulatant, I have strong personal skills and a strong understanding of how businesses work.
