About Me

My name is Tom Frost and I'm a self-taught web developer based in Oxfordshire, UK. Having previously worked as a Travel Consultant, I have excellent personal skills and a strong understanding of how businesses work; however, I realised that I no longer saw my future in the travel industry. Therefore, in December 2019, I decided to pursue my dream of learning computer programming and web development, with the ultimate goal of transitioning into a career in web development.

So far my journey has been fixated on front-end technologies: I recently finished an advanced course on CSS and Sass, and am currently focusing on Javascript and the React Framework. Beyond that, I intend to venture into back-end technology and do a deep dive into C# and .NET. Over the past 6 months I have developed a real passion for web development, and am committed to continuing to improve my code, learn new technologies and skills and expand my knowledge. Here, I hope to share with you just some of what I have learnt on my journey so far.
